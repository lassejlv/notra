Connect your Notra account to Kontentspace via the Notra MCP in just a few steps.

## Setup

1. **Create an API key** in your Notra workspace under the **API keys** section.
   - Choose between read-only or read-write access.

2. **Open [Kontentspace connections](https://kontentspace.com/settings/connections)** and click **Connect** on the Notra MCP integration.

3. **Enter your API key** in the dialog and select the access level.
   - Read-only grants view access.
   - Read-write allows the AI agent to modify data.

That's it — your Notra account is now connected to Kontentspace.
